About us

Hardware Repair is a business dedicated to repairing faults in all types of computers, including desktop PCs, laptops, and mobile phones. We offer a range of repair options including data recovery, hardware upgrades, and more. We strive to provide the utmost in customer support and in the quality of the repairs that we provide.

Hardware Repair specialises in providing low-cost and high quality repairs for a wide range of devices. Requests for repair jobs can easily be made online, and a unique number will be provided to you, to allow you to keep track of how the repair's going.

When the repair's done, you can either come and collect locally or have your device shipped back to you. You can always be sure that the repair will be good quality and we always aim to complete repairs in good time. If, for whatever reason, a repair is not able to be completed, then you will not be charged.
